    How the engine works

    1) Seed

    Start from your top tracks + Spotify audio features (energy, danceability, valence, acousticness, tempo).

    2) Adapt

    Each Save / Keep / Skip applies a weighted delta to the taste vector, clamped to 0–1 and normalized.

    3) Rank

    Compute cosine similarity between the taste vector and available tracks; pick the best-scoring option and avoid repeats.

    4) Explain

    Surface the dominant overlaps (e.g., high energy + tempo, or acoustic + warm valence) so the user sees why it fits.
